Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026 | Effective date: May 24, 2026

JingHu processes spam detection entirely on your device. Your call logs, SMS content, and contacts never leave your phone.

Hubu Tech Co., Ltd. ("we") operates the JingHu mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Locally Processed Information (Never Uploaded)

Data TypePurposeStorage
Call log metadataIdentify spam callsDevice only
SMS contentAnalyze spam/phishingDevice only
Phone numbers (hashed)Match spam number databaseDevice only
Blocking rulesExecute user preferencesDevice only
AI model inference dataClassify spam messagesDevice only

1.2 Information Collected With Your Consent

Data TypePurposeLegal Basis
Anonymous usage statisticsImprove app performanceConsent (Firebase Analytics)
Crash reportsFix bugs, improve stabilityConsent (Firebase Crashlytics)
Premium subscription dataProcess paymentsContract

We never collect the following:

  • Your contacts
  • Complete phone numbers (cloud queries use irreversible hashing only)
  • SMS content (100% on-device processing)
  • Call recordings
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers for tracking

2. Cloud Queries

When JingHu queries the cloud spam number database:

  • Your phone creates an irreversible SHA-256 hash of the phone number
  • Only the hash value is sent to our server
  • The server returns a spam score for that hash
  • The original phone number is never transmitted

3. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • SQLCipher Encryption: Local database uses AES-256 encryption
  • Android Keystore: Keys derived from device hardware features, securely stored
  • TLS 1.3: All network communication uses the latest encryption protocol
  • Certificate Pinning: Prevents man-in-the-middle attacks
  • R8 Code Obfuscation: Prevents reverse engineering

4. Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained on your device until you delete the app or manually clear it
  • Crash reports: Retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted
  • Anonymous statistics: Retained for 26 months, then automatically deleted
  • Subscription data: Retained for 3 years after subscription ends (legal requirement)

5. Your Rights

Under applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Right to Know: Understand what personal information we collect
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing (e.g., analytics)
  • Right to Portability: Obtain your data in a structured format

To exercise these rights, contact: developer@gdpp.com

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected children's data, we will delete it immediately.
